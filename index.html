Protocol Mechanics

Four steps to
fractional ownership.

A transparent, onchain process from asset listing to yield distribution.

01
Asset Listing
Asset owners submit real-world assets with documentation, valuation, and yield parameters. The Propex DAO reviews and approves listings onchain.
02
Fractionalization
Approved assets are tokenized into a configurable number of fractions, each representing a proportional claim on the underlying asset and its yield.
03
Trade & Hold
Buy fractions directly from the pool or via the secondary market. Sell anytime. All trades are settled onchain in CNPY with a 1.5% platform fee.
04
Earn & Govern
Collect proportional yield distributions from asset revenue. Vote on governance proposals — your fraction count = your voting weight.
